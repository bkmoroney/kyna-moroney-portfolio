About Me

I’m a senior graphic designer with over 5 years of experience local to the Chicago area. I’m somewhat of a jack of all trades with experience ranging from in-house marketing, to website design, to local freelance projects.

 

I’m always eager to jump into projects that require me to learn something new. Whether it’s new subject matter, a new tool, or a new style, I have the ability to tailor my skillset and quick learning mentality to adapt to the needs of the project.

 

When I’m not designing, I have way too many hobbies including digital art, sewing, and spending time with my canine niece and nephew.
